About

Cosmetic Sim is a fan-made, browser-based simulator inspired by Blooket’s cosmetic and case opening features.

This project is not affiliated with Blooket or any official game. It's created for fun, No servers, no accounts, no real prizes—just open cases and collect virtual cosmetics for your inventory.

  • All data is saved locally in your browser.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • All original artwork and code (unless otherwise credited).
